Privacy Policy

CSV Clean Inspector respects your privacy.

Data Processing

All data processing is performed locally in your browser. CSV files are never uploaded to any server.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

No Tracking

This extension does not use analytics, tracking, or third-party services.

Local Storage

The extension may use Chrome's local storage to save temporary application state (such as loaded CSV data and transformation steps). This data remains on your device and is not shared.

Third-Party Access

No third parties have access to your data.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact the developer.
